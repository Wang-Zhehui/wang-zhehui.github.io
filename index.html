Zhehui Wang (王哲辉)

I am an Assistant Professor at Great Bay University.
My research interests lie in geometric analysis and partial differential equations.
I was a postdoc member at Academy of Mathematics and Systems Science of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, under the sponsorship of Feimin Huang.
I completed my Ph.D. at Peking University in July 2021, under the supervision of Qing Han.
I was a visiting student at University of Notre Dame from September 2019 to November 2020.
I was an undergraduate student at Xiamen University from September 2012 to July 2016. Actually I changed my major from Physics to Mathematics in March 2013.
